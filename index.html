About Me

Hi there! I’m Marinkie, a software developer with a passion for crafting clean and functional web applications. My journey into coding began with a fascination for how websites work behind the scenes, and since then, I’ve dedicated myself to mastering front-end development. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Limpopo, where I developed a solid foundation in programming and problem-solving. Over time, I’ve become proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Vue.js, using these tools to build responsive and user-friendly websites.

What excites me most about development is the continuous learning it offers. I’m always exploring new technologies and frameworks to enhance my skill set and create even better digital experiences. Whether it's a small personal project or collaborating on a team, I bring creativity, attention to detail, and a relentless drive to every line of code I write. When I’m not coding, you can find me hanging with my family, or "exploring the latest tech trends" or just working on personal side projects. I’m excited to grow my career in software development and contribute to projects that make a real impact.
